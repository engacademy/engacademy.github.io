    Our Story

    How We Got Started

    EngAcademy was founded by Rithvik Chuppala. He strongly believes that learning is the main way for low-income to escape their less-fortunate circumstances. He also strongly believes in giving back to the community that helped him become who he is. At first, he led a group of students who went to a local schools and coordinated classes that taught children basic robotics, programming, engineering, and physics through a project-based curriculum.

    The Next Steps...

    We soon broadened our outreach to several public and private schools around the Silicon Valley area and expanded to over 15 student volunteers. We also plan on registering our organization as a 501c3 in the coming future.

    Where We Are Now

    With these dedicated members who have a great passion for helping students and giving back to the community, we will continue to strive for our mission of both educating less-fortunate students and giving back to our local community

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to both educate less-fortunate students in order to level the playing field between them and their peers but also to give back to the local community.
